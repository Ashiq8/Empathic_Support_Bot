TechMinds Electronics - Customer Support Policy & Refund Rules

1. REFUND PERIOD:
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in original packaging.
If the request is made after 30 days but before 60 days, only 50% refund is applicable.
No refunds are allowed after 60 days.

2. WARRANTY:
All laptops and phones come with a 1-year hardware warranty.
Physical damage (e.g., cracked screen, water damage) is NOT covered under warranty.
Software issues are covered for 6 months.

3. REPLACEMENT:
If a device is dead-on-arrival (DOA), it will be replaced instantly within 7 days.
For replacement, the original invoice is mandatory.

4. SUPPORT HOURS:
Our support team is available from 9 AM to 6 PM EST, Monday to Friday.
Email support response time is 24 hours.
